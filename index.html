About Me

    Matthew Williams is a Full-Stack Web Developer, with a background in graphic design and brand management. He is a recent graduate of University of Pennsylvania's full-stack development program, and has earned a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Multimedia Design from Duquesne University. His background and career experiences in NYC/Brooklyn and Philadelphia in front end design, along with his back end server-side and database knowledge, allows Matt to be fluent and productive in all apsects of the design process.

Matt is fueled by his passion for sharp and fresh designs, as well as being able to write clear and consice code to add functionality and creative apsects to each project or application he is involved in. He is constantly coding, drawing and learning new languages, eager to learn and build on his academic and professional foundation as a well-rounded full-stack developer.

His determination and creative mindset has contributed to his most recent success as a graduate of the UPenn full-stack program, and his position before that as a successful real estate salesperson and media manager at his firm in Philadelphia. There, he led his firm's team with over $2.5 mil in net sales, as well as handling the creating of all marketing design materials, including emails, advertisements, mailers, and signs for owned and managed buidlings.

Previously as a freelance brand manager, graphic designer, and even as an associate water inspector for the NYC DEP, at each position Matt has held, he has vastly improved the productivity of his department by implementing strategic project management methods, and streamlining technological processes to save time and improve projects. He believes hard work and loyalty in the workplace is key to success, and lives these ideals out by always striving to learn and improve himself and his skills.

Matt is currently working as a freelance developer,and is looking for a new full-time or contract challenge to continue his passion and put his skills to use creating functional and eye-catching apps and sites. Reach out to Matt at MattWills09@gmail.com to connect!
